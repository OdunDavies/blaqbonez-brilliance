CHAPTER 01 — ORIGIN

The Story

Born Emeka Akumefule on January 29, 1996, in Imo State, Nigeria — raised in a Yoruba and Urhobo mixed environment, shaped by two worlds. At 13, a song on the radio changed everything.

By 16, he'd beaten 3,000 rappers in Terry Tha Rapman's Zombie Competition. By 2019, The New York Times called him one of the new guards of Nigerian music. Signed to Chocolate City. Studied Computer Engineering at OAU.

Declared himself the Best Rapper in Africa — and dared anyone to prove him wrong.
